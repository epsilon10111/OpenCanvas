\section*{Scenario 1 Analysis: Automation of Repetitive Experiments}

\subsection*{Problem Statement and Design Analysis}
Based on the provided scenario regarding physics and chemistry experiments, the following analysis outlines the core problems, boundary conditions, needs, and proposed solutions to mitigate the time consumption and staff exhaustion associated with high-volume sample measurements.

\begin{table}[h!]
\centering
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.5}
\setlist[itemize]{noitemsep, topsep=0pt, leftmargin=*}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{|X|X|}
\hline
\textbf{Problem(s):} & \textbf{Boundary Conditions:} \\ \hline
\begin{itemize}
    \item High time consumption per measurement (up to 2 hours).
    \item Staff exhaustion due to high volume of samples.
    \item Repetitive manual setup procedures.
    \item Potential for human error during prolonged tasks.
\end{itemize} & 
\begin{itemize}
    \item Experiment methods are conventional.
    \item Limited varying parameters for setup.
    \item Must comply with laboratory safety standards.
    \item Compatibility with existing instrumentation.
    \item Budget constraints for implementation.
\end{itemize} \\ \hline
\textbf{Needs:} & \textbf{Solutions:} \\ \hline
\begin{itemize}
    \item Automation of repetitive measurement tasks.
    \item Reduction of direct human intervention.
    \item Increased throughput efficiency.
    \item Improved ergonomic conditions for staff.
    \item Reliable automated data logging.
\end{itemize} & 
\begin{itemize}
    \item Implement robotic sample handling systems.
    \item Develop script-controlled instrumentation setup.
    \item Utilize parallel processing stations for multiple samples.
    \item Integrate automated data acquisition software.
    \item Design modular fixtures for quick changeovers.
\end{itemize} \\ \hline

    \item \textbf{Member 1:} Problem identification and needs analysis.
    \item \textbf{Member 2:} Boundary condition definition and solution brainstorming.
    \item \textbf{Member 3:} Documentation and final report compilation.
    \item \textbf{All Members:} Collaborative review and validation.
\end{itemize} & 
\\ \hline
\end{tabularx}
